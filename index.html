// Frequently Asked Questions //

Get answers to common questions about G-Browser

Is G-Browser really completely free? +

Absolutely! G-Browser is 100% free with no hidden costs, subscriptions, or premium tiers. Everything you see is available at zero cost.

How effective is the built-in ad-blocker? +

Our ad-blocker uses 134,000+ filter rules and blocks approximately 95% of ads, including YouTube ads, popups, banners, and tracking scripts. It works silently in the background.

Can I import bookmarks from Chrome/Firefox? +

Bookmark import is planned for Version 2! In the current version (v1.0), you'll need to manually add your favorite websites to bookmarks.

Is my browsing data safe and private? +

100% private. We don't collect ANY personal data. Your bookmarks, history, and settings are stored locally on your computer. No tracking, no analytics, no data selling.

What features are planned for future versions? +

G-Browser v2 will include: Download manager UI, Enhanced ad-blocking, Themes/skins, Extensions support, Password manager, and Mac/Linux versions.

How can I support G-Browser development? +

You can: 1) Use G-Browser and share with friends, 2) Report bugs or suggest features, 3) Contribute code on GitHub, 4) Spread the word on social media.
